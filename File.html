Depression Assessment Quiz

Please select one option for each question.

Questions:

1. How often do you feel sad or depressed?

2. Do you have difficulty finding joy in activities you once enjoyed?

3. How often do you feel fatigued or low on energy?

4. Are you having trouble sleeping (insomnia) or sleeping too much?

5. Have you experienced a change in appetite or weight?

6. Do you find it difficult to concentrate or make decisions?

7. How often do you feel worthless or have feelings of guilt?

8. Have you withdrawn from social activities or avoided spending time with friends and family?

9. How often do you feel overwhelmed by your responsibilities?

10. Do you experience physical symptoms like headaches or stomachaches without a clear cause?

11. How often do you feel anxious or restless?

12. Do you find it hard to relax or unwind?

13. How often do you have thoughts of self-harm or suicide?

14. Have you noticed a lack of motivation to perform daily tasks?

15. Do you feel disconnected from reality or like you're living in a fog?
